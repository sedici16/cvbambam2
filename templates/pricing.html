Common questions

What counts as a CV?

Each uploaded file (PDF or Word) counts as one CV. The free tier allows 10 in total across all time. Pro removes this limit entirely.

How does the Pro upgrade work?

Pay via the Wise link on the upgrade page, submit your payment reference, and your account is upgraded within one business day. No card details stored on our end.

Is my candidate data private?

Yes. Your talent pool is scoped to your account only. No candidate data is shared between users or used to train any models.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes. There are no long-term contracts. Contact us and we'll cancel your subscription with no questions asked.

I'm a recruitment agency — is there a plan for me?

Yes — the Custom plan is built for agencies and teams that need multiple user accounts and a shared talent pool. Get in touch and we'll put something together.
