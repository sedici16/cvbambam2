How Instant CV Parsing Works

Step 1 — Upload Your CVs

Drag and drop your PDF or Word CV files directly into the tool. Batch upload is supported — upload all your applications at once rather than one at a time. The tool accepts the formats candidates actually use.

Step 2 — Enter Your Job Description

Paste your job description — or the key requirements for the role — into the text field. The AI uses this to understand what you're looking for and ranks every candidate against it semantically. Not just keywords: actual meaning and relevance.

Step 3 — Get Ranked Candidates Instantly

Within seconds, every uploaded CV has been parsed, structured, and scored. The candidates are ranked from highest to lowest semantic match. Your most relevant candidates are at the top — you don't have to read through the pile to find them.

Step 4 — Export to CSV and Share

One click downloads all candidate data as a CSV file — names, emails, skills, experience, match scores, and more. Open it in Google Sheets, share it with co-founders or team members, add your own notes, and build your shortlist from structured data rather than a pile of PDFs.
